Roofing Crew Leader / Site Supervisor

🏢 TopClad Metal Roofing 📍 Halifax, Nova Scotia 🕐 Full-Time · Year-Round
💰 $35–$48/hr + Profit Sharing + Full Benefits Package

We're not your typical roofing company.

TopClad is one of Atlantic Canada's fastest-growing metal roofing companies — and we're looking for a leader, not just a worker. If you're the person your current crew actually listens to, we want to talk.

What's in it for you

  • $35–$48/hr depending on experience — plus profit sharing
  • Full benefits package (health, dental, vision)
  • Year-round, stable employment — no seasonal layoffs
  • Real career path: crew leader → management → ownership opportunities
  • A crew that actually has your back
  • Work that lasts — 40-year roofs you'll be proud of
  • Hats, sunscreen, and a great outdoor work environment

What you'll do

  • Lead a 3–5 person residential metal roofing crew across Halifax & surrounding areas
  • Manage daily site operations, quality, and safety standards
  • Coach and develop junior roofers on technique and trade skills
  • Coordinate with scheduling and clients to keep projects on track
  • Uphold TopClad's standard for quality — the best roofs in Nova Scotia

What we're looking for

  • 3+ years roofing or carpentry experience
  • Experience leading or supervising a crew (or ready to step up)
  • Strong work ethic and a great attitude
  • Working at Heights certification (or willing to obtain)
  • Valid driver's license

Why TopClad?

We're locally sourced, Halifax-based, and we're building something real here. Our materials are sourced right in Nova Scotia — steel with zinc and aluminum alloy coating with a 40-year warranty. Our people grow fast. Our roofs last a lifetime. Our team culture is built on trust, fun, and getting it done right.

We're a proud supporter of Construction Safety NS and WCB Nova Scotia.

👉 We respond to every application. Don't wait.
