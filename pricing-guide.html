📈 Market Trends (2024 → 2026)

Key Takeaways

  • Construction materials up ~15–25% overall vs. 2024 ↑ Rising
  • Aluminum up 30%+ year-over-year — four-year highs in the US market
  • Steel bars up ~12% YoY; domestic mill prices still climbing
  • US tariffs up to 50% on some imported metals — pushing domestic prices higher too
  • Paint & coating costs rising across all substrates and vendors
  • Labour shortages continue — skilled roofer wages trending up 10–20%
  • Some Canadian sources forecast slight stabilization in late 2026 Stabilizing?
  • Sheffield Metals (Feb 2026): tight supply, no near-term price relief expected

Additional Cost Items

  • Underlayment: Synthetic $0.25–$0.65/sq ft; ice & water shield $0.75–$1.50/sq ft
  • Flashing & trim: $2–$5/linear ft (valleys, drip edge, rake, ridge cap)
  • Fasteners & sealants: $0.15–$0.40/sq ft
  • Old roof tear-off: $1.00–$2.50/sq ft (1 layer asphalt)
  • Dump fees: $300–$600 per job (varies by municipality)
  • Snow guards: $1.50–$3.00/linear ft where required

Quoting Notes

  • If hip roof instead of gable, add ~5% to area (~1,412 sq ft before waste)
  • 4:12 pitch is walkable — no steep-pitch premium needed
  • Confirm number of existing shingle layers for tear-off pricing
  • Check soffit/fascia condition — may need repair/replacement
  • Include ridge vent or other ventilation in quote
  • Get current supplier pricing before finalizing — these are market averages
